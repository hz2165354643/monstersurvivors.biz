About Monster Survivors

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an adrenaline-pumping survival action game where you face wave after wave of nightmarish creatures. Set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by monsters, your goal is simple yet challenging: survive as long as possible while defeating monsters and collecting power-ups to strengthen your character.

With its roguelike elements, each playthrough offers a unique experience with different upgrades, enemy patterns, and challenges to overcome. How long can you survive the monster apocalypse?

Tips & Tricks

Beginner Tips

Focus on movement and dodging first. Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Early game upgrades that increase your speed or area damage are usually the most beneficial for newcomers.

Advanced Strategies

Plan your build ahead of time and choose upgrades that complement each other. Some abilities have powerful synergy effects when combined. Don't neglect defensive upgrades - survival is just as important as dealing damage.

Boss Encounters

Bosses have predictable attack patterns - learn to recognize the visual cues before their attacks. Clear nearby smaller monsters before focusing on the boss to give yourself more room to maneuver.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes, Monster Survivors is completely free to play directly in your web browser. No downloads or accounts required!

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes, Monster Survivors is optimized for both desktop and mobile play. On mobile devices, touch controls will automatically appear for easy gameplay.

Does my progress save between sessions?

The game saves your high scores and achievements using browser local storage. To ensure your progress is saved, play on the same device and browser, and avoid clearing your browser data.

How do I unlock new characters?

New characters are unlocked by achieving specific milestones in the game, such as surviving for a certain amount of time or defeating special boss monsters. Keep playing to discover all available characters!
